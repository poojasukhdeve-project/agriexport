Event

Dated 31 December 2021 to 1 January 2022

Export Vegetables from LBSI Airport Varanasi to Dubai UAE, with the cooperation of Farmer Producing company, Apeda and GMO held inauguration function to initiate export. Expand our operations and market reach to increase our mutual economy. On this occasion, Ms Aryama Sanyal Director of Airport Authority of India was invited as a chief dignity along with Mr. Munna Yadav Deputy Director as chief guest, in the presence of Mr Shubham ji Apeda. Mr Pravin Wankhade owner of Universe Exports, Rakesh Dubey Indigo Authority and dignitaries were present. With the cooperation of Arone International exports, New India export, Agricos export and Farmer Producer Company, the event was successful.
